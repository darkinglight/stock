## 1. 巴菲特对 Schloss 的完整评价

### 1.1 《格雷厄姆-多德都市的超级投资者》(1984)

**原文摘录** (来源: Hermes, Columbia Business School Magazine, Fall 1984):

> "Walter never went to college, but took a course from Ben Graham at night at the New York Institute of Finance. Walter left Graham-Newman in 1955 and achieved the record shown here over 28 years."

> "He has no connections or access to useful information. Practically no one in Wall Street knows him and he is not fed any ideas. He looks up the numbers in the manuals and sends for the annual reports, and that's about it."

> "In introducing me to (Schloss) Warren had also, to my mind, described himself. 'He never forgets that he is handling other people's money, and this reinforces his normal strong aversion to loss.' He has total integrity and a realistic picture of himself. Money is real to him and stocks are real -- and from this flows an attraction to the 'margin of safety' principle."

> "Walter has diversified enormously, owning well over 100 stocks currently. He knows how to identify securities that sell at considerably less than their value to a private owner. **And that's all he does.** He doesn't worry about whether it's January, he doesn't worry about whether it's Monday, he doesn't worry about whether it's an election year. He simply says, if a business is worth a dollar and I can buy it for 40 cents, something good may happen to me. And he does it over and over and over again."

> "He owns many more stocks than I do -- and is far less interested in the underlying nature of the business; I don't seem to have very much influence on Walter. That's one of his strengths; **no one has much influence on him.** "

- **来源URL:** https://business.columbia.edu/cgi-finance/chazen-global-insights/superinvestors-graham-and-doddsville
- **可信度:** 极高（巴菲特原文，哥大官方档案）

### 1.2 2006 年致股东信中的评价

**原文摘录** (来源: Berkshire Hathaway 2006 Annual Letter):

> "I first publicly discussed Walter's remarkable record in 1984. At that time 'efficient market theory' (EMT) was the centerpiece of investment instruction at most major business schools... When I talked about Walter 23 years ago, his record forcefully contradicted this dogma."

> "And what did members of the academic community do when they were exposed to this new and important evidence? Unfortunately, they reacted in all-too-human fashion: Rather than opening their minds, they closed their eyes. To my knowledge no business school teaching EMT made any attempt to study Walter's performance and what it meant for the school's cherished theory."

> "Tens of thousands of students were therefore sent out into life believing that on every day the price of every stock was 'right'... Walter meanwhile went on overperforming, his job made easier by the misguided instructions that had been given to those young minds. After all, if you are in the shipping business, it's helpful to have all of your potential competitors be taught that the earth is flat."

> "Maybe it was a good thing for his investors that Walter didn't go to college."

**关于风险：**
> "[Schloss took] no real risk, defined as permanent loss of capital... [he invested] in about 1,000 securities, mostly of a lackluster type. A few big winners did not account for his success."

- **来源URL:** https://hedgefundalpha.com/walter-schloss/ (引用巴菲特致股东信)
- **可信度:** 极高

### 1.3 1976 年致"巴菲特集团"信中的评价

> "...armed only with a monthly stock guide, a sophisticated style acquired largely from association with me, a sub-lease on a portion of a closet at Tweedy, Browne and a group of partners... Walter strode forth to do battle with the S&P..."

- **来源URL:** https://www.stockopedia.com/content/the-superinvestors-of-graham-and-doddsville-walter-schloss-90668/
- **可信度:** 高

### 1.4 分类评价

| 维度 | 巴菲特的措辞 | 语气 |
|------|------------|------|
| 智力/教育 | "never went to college" | 中立偏褒义 |
| 方法 | "And that's all he does" (重复强调) | 赞赏其专注 |
| 性格 | "no one has much influence on him" | 高度赞赏 |
| 风险 | "no real risk, defined as permanent loss of capital" | 非常认可 |
| 对学术界的意义 | 对有效市场理论的活体反驳 | 利用其业绩作武器 |

## 2. 其他价值投资者对 Schloss 的看法

### 2.1 Seth Klarman

Klarman 没有直接评价 Schloss 本人的公开记录，但 Klarman 的投资哲学与 Schloss 高度一致：

> "Price is the essential determinant in every investment equation. At some price, every company is a buy; at some price, every company is a hold; and at a still higher price, every company is a sell."

> "A margin of safety is achieved when securities are purchased at prices sufficiently below underlying value to allow for human error, bad luck, or extreme volatility in a complex, unpredictable, and rapidly changing world."

Klarman 在 MIT 2007 年演讲中引用巴菲特关于"格雷厄姆-多德都市超级投资者"的论述，虽然没有点名 Schloss，但将其视为广义的格雷厄姆式投资者的成功范例。

- **来源URL:** https://pdfcoffee.com/seth-klarman-mit-speech-2007-pdf-free.html
- **可信度:** 高

### 2.2 Howard Marks (橡树资本)

Marks 没有直接评价 Schloss，但在其备忘录 *Something of Value* (2021) 和 *The Calculus of Value* 中对"捡烟蒂"策略做了经典描述：

> "Deemed 'cigar butt' investing by his protege Warren Buffett, Graham's style emphasized the search for pedestrian companies whose shares were selling at discounts from liquidation value based on the assets on their balance sheets, which Buffett likened to searching the street for used cigar butts that had one last puff left in them."

Marks 认为捡烟蒂策略的时代已过去，但"价值"不等于"低估值指标"：

> "The rules of the past that constrain the value industry, ratios like price-to-book, price-to-earnings... were the sine qua non of value investing but not as relevant in the new world."

> "There's no reason why a fast-growing company can't be good value or why a low multiple company can't be a good investment."

- **来源URL:** https://www.oaktreecapital.com/insights/memo/something-of-value
- **可信度:** 极高

### 2.3 James Montier (GMO)

Montier 在 *Value Investing: Tools and Techniques for Intelligent Investment* (2009) 中明确将 Schloss 列为格雷厄姆策略的成功实践者，并实证检验了 net-net 策略：

> "Testing such a deep value approach reveals that it would have been a highly profitable strategy. Over the period 1985-2007, buying a global basket of net-nets would have generated a return of over 35% p.a. versus an equally weighted universe return of 17% p.a."

Montier 同时指出 net-net 策略的核心缺点：容量有限、个股层面高失败率（约 5% 的个股年跌幅超过 90%）、流动性差。

- **来源URL:** https://greenbackd.com/2010/01/21/montier-on-net-nets-a-simple-quantitative-value-strategy/
- **可信度:** 高

### 2.4 Christopher Browne (Tweedy, Browne)

Tweedy, Browne 公司与 Schloss 有长期合作关系——Schloss 使用 Tweedy, Browne 的办公室达数十年。

> Tweedy, Browne 的官方网站目前将自己描述为 "Built on value-oriented investment methodologies first pioneered by Benjamin Graham, Warren Buffett, Charles Munger, and **Walter Schloss**, with whom the firm was associated in various capacities at times during its long history."

Tweedy, Browne 出版的 *What Has Worked in Investing* 手册收集了 50 多项学术研究，证明了低市净率、低估值、net-net 策略的历史有效性——间接印证了 Schloss 方法论的合理性。

- **来源URL:** https://www.tweedy.com/
- **可信度:** 极高

### 2.5 Bruce Greenwald (哥伦比亚大学)

Greenwald 在 *Value Investing: From Graham to Buffett and Beyond* (2001) 中分析 Schloss 的策略：

> "The Schlosses would rather trust their own analysis and their longstanding commitment to buying cheap stocks... This approach leads them to focus almost exclusively on the published financial statements that public firms must produce each quarter. They start by looking at the balance sheet. Can they buy the company for less than the value of the assets, net of all debt? If so, the stock is a candidate for purchase."

- **来源URL:** https://www.investmentnews.com/ria-news/legendary-graham-disciple-and-buffett-favorite-dead-at-95/42387
- **可信度:** 高

### 2.6 其他相关人物

| 人物 | 关系 | 要点 |
|------|------|------|
| **Charles Munger** | 同为巴菲特口中的超级投资者 | 未找到 Munger 对 Schloss 的直接评价。Munger 是"用合理价格买伟大企业"的倡导者，天然对立于纯捡烟蒂策略。这种沉默本身可视为一种立场 |
| **Bill Ruane** (Sequoia Fund) | 同为超级投资者 | 无直接评价记录 |
| **Tom Knapp** (Tweedy, Browne 合伙人) | 同为格雷厄姆-纽曼出身 | 无直接评价记录 |
| **Irving Kahn** (Kahn Brothers) | 同为格雷厄姆学生 | Kahn 也终生践行格雷厄姆方法，与 Schloss 被视为最忠实的格雷厄姆追随者 |

## 3. 财经媒体的分析与报道

### 3.1 Forbes (1973)

Forbes 杂志在报道中将 Schloss 描述为：

> "Walter Schloss, 57, is kind of a junk collector among stock market players. He is not much interested in earnings growth or in management or in other things that concern most analysts. He's only interested in cheap stocks."

> "Schloss recognizes that he won't find any Xeroxes on his bargain counter -- but no Levitzes either. 'I just want to grow 15% to 20% a year, and my average is 17%. I'll take my profit when it comes.'"

- **来源URL:** https://acquirersmultiple.com/2017/06/walter-schloss-cagr/
- **可信度:** 高

### 3.2 "Adam Smith" (George J.W. Goodman) - *Supermoney* (1972)

> "He has no connections or access to useful information. Practically no one in Wall Street knows him and he is not fed any ideas."

这篇文章由巴菲特引荐后撰写，是华尔街最早对 Schloss 的外界报道。

- **来源URL:** 同上 (巴菲特演讲中引用)
- **可信度:** 高

### 3.3 Barron's (1985)

Schloss 在 Barron's 采访中谈论了与格雷厄姆共事的经历以及自己的投资哲学。

- **来源URL:** https://novelinvestor.com/walter-schloss-ben-graham-value-works/
- **可信度:** 高

### 3.4 中文财经媒体

东方证券资产管理公司转载的巴菲特评价中写道：

> "他从来不用电脑，但坚持每天早上读报，唯一其他参考资料是 Value Line 杂志，最喜欢看'表现最差股票列表'... 1956年自立门户后，将近50年年复合收益率16%。难怪，Forbes杂志曾在封面说，听Schloss谈'简单'的价值投资对现代人是一剂'补药'"

- **来源URL:** https://www.dfham.com/contents/2014/8/28-81de433ab2344f03b6d80fad8531ef2a.html
- **可信度:** 中高（转载）

### 3.5 Financial History (MoAF, Fall 2021)

博物馆 of American Finance 详细回顾了 Schloss 的一生，包括他的16条投资原则。其中关键分析：

> "It would be problematic to apply a simple price/book ratio in today's market, given the dominance of intangible assets in many companies' asset valuation."

Edwin Schloss 在采访中指出：

> "The market environment has changed substantially since he and Walter closed the partnership in 2002. Some companies with seemingly attractive price/book or price/earnings ratios may be 'value traps.'"

- **来源URL:** http://www.fordhamgabellicenter.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/Walter-Schloss.pdf
- **可信度:** 极高（学术期刊）

## 4. 现代策略争议：net-net / 捡烟蒂是否已失效

### 4.1 正方：策略已基本失效

**观点 A: 市场结构变化 (Howard Marks / Oaktree)**

> "In Graham's day, cigar butt opportunities could be found in good supply, valued precisely, bought very cheaply with confidence, and then sold once the price had risen... But as the world evolved, the landscape of opportunities has changed significantly."

**观点 B: 信息优势消失 (Glen Bradford, 2026)**

> "In 1934, a Graham-style analyst had an edge simply by reading 10-K filings that most investors ignored. In 2026, every 10-K is parsed by dozens of algorithms within seconds of filing. The informational edge Graham exploited in small-cap net-nets is largely gone in developed markets."

> "In developed markets, net-nets are mostly value traps."

- **来源URL:** https://glenbradford.com/security-analysis-modern-application
- **可信度:** 中高

**观点 C: 策略在牛市中无效 (Benevolus Capital, 2025 回测)**

Benevolus Capital 回测了 2004-2024 年美股的 net-net 策略，发现：

| 指标 | 结果 |
|------|------|
| 算术平均回报 | +55% |
| 实际总回报 (几何) | **-44.04%** |
| 最高年回报 | 177.18% (2009年) |
| 最差年回报 | -50.98% (2014年) |

结论：**"modern cigar butt investing only really works in distressed markets."** 在牛市和低利率环境下，net-net 筛选出的往往是"undergoing massive share dilution, speculative companies, fraudulent companies or ones that have a huge cash burn."

- **来源URL:** https://benevoluscapital.com/2025/02/12/us-public-equity-research-backtesting-ben-grahams-net-net-investment-strategy-in-us-markets-from-2004-2024/
- **可信度:** 中高（透明方法论、承认偏差）

**观点 D: 无形资产时代使市净率标准过时**

> "Modern companies' greatest strengths are often intangible assets that aren't accurately reflected by book value. Think of today's technology giants, for instance." (Monevator, 2020)

- **来源URL:** https://monevator.com/walter-schloss/
- **可信度:** 中

### 4.2 反方：策略仍然有效，但需调整

**观点 A: 在小市值和国际化市场中仍存在 (Mr Deep Value, 2025)**

> "Net-net or near net-net... Academic work continues to find net-current-asset strategies profitable in various markets and periods, though they can be capacity-constrained and lumpy."

> "Schloss's edge came from behaviour and breadth, not secrets. Those edges still exist because: Small and micro-cap shares remain under-researched... Humans still extrapolate bad news and over-react to short-term results."

- **来源URL:** https://www.mrdeepvalue.com/p/how-to-invest-like-walter-schloss
- **可信度:** 中

**观点 B: 加上质量筛选后仍有效 (Focus on Risks, 2025)**

> "The Net-Net Deep Value screener, when rigorously applied and augmented with modern solvency filters (Altman Z-Score >2.99, positive FCF), remains one of the most statistically advantageous strategies in finance."

- **来源URL:** https://focusonrisksv.substack.com/p/net-net-deep-value-a-quantitative
- **可信度:** 中

**观点 C: Cliff Asness / AQR 的研究**

AQR 的研究表明，虽然纯 net-net 机会减少，但 "value factor" (低PB、低PE) 在全球范围内长期有效。Schloss 的策略可被视为对价值因子的一种极端化暴露。

- **可信度:** 高（机构研究）

### 4.3 Schloss 本人的演进

Schloss 自己也在不断调整。他在采访中承认：

> "Yes, it's changed because the market's changed. I can't buy any working capital stocks anymore so instead of saying well I can't buy 'em, I'm not going to play the game, you have to decide what you want to do."

于是他从买净运营资本股转向买低于账面价值的股票。这体现了一个重要事实：**即使是最坚定的捡烟蒂践行者，也在随市场演变而调整。**

- **来源URL:** https://www.thegoodinvestors.sg/an-unknown-investing-giants-walter-schloss-fascinating-insights/
- **可信度:** 高

## 5. 与同时代投资者的对比分析

### 5.1 Schloss vs. Warren Buffett

| 维度 | Walter Schloss | Warren Buffett |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| **教育** | 未上大学，仅参加格雷厄姆夜课 | 哥大本科 + 格雷厄姆亲传 A+ |
| **方法** | 纯数量化，只看财务报表 | 质量 + 数量结合，深入了解企业 |
| **与管理层关系** | 几乎不接触 | 深入接触，甚至收购控制权 |
| **集中度** | 高度分散 (100+ 只股票) | 高度集中 (前5持仓常占 80%+) |
| **持股周期** | 约 4 年 (换手率 25%) | "永远持有" (部分持仓) |
| **企业理解** | "Warren understands businesses -- I don't" | 深度理解护城河和管理层 |
| **规模管理** | 限制规模，最多 92 个投资者 | 不断扩大 |
| **进化** | 从 net-net 到低市净率 | 从捡烟蒂到"合理价格买伟大企业" |
| **年化回报(约)** | 15-16% (净，45年) | 20%+ (伯克希尔，37年) |
| **关键优势** | 纪律、谦虚、知限 | 商业判断力、识人能力 |

Schloss 自己的总结：

> "Warren is a brilliant, he's not only a good analyst, but he's a very good judge of businesses... But you've got to know your limitations."

**来源URL:** https://www.thegoodinvestors.sg/an-unknown-investing-giants-walter-schloss-fascinating-insights/

### 5.2 Schloss vs. Tweedy, Browne

| 维度 | Walter Schloss | Tweedy, Browne |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| **起源** | 格雷厄姆-纽曼分析师 | 早期做市商，后转为投资管理 |
| **策略** | 纯被动捡烟蒂，不参与管理 | 偶尔取得控制权、参与催化 |
| **规模** | 极小 ($4M-$50M) | 较大 ($130亿+) |
| **员工** | 1-2 人 (本人+儿子) | 11+ 人投资团队 |
| **研究方式** | Value Line + 年报邮寄 | 深度研究、与管理层沟通 |
| **信息来源** | 公开财务数据 | 公开+直接沟通+行业研究 |

Tweedy, Browne 的操作更像"升级版"的 Schloss 策略——保留了捡烟蒂的核心，但增加了催化剂驱动和控制权策略。

### 5.3 Schloss vs. Peter Lynch

Lynch 的 Fidelity Magellan 基金也持有很多股票（有时上千只），但方法论完全不同：

- Lynch：拜访数百家公司/年，关注增长故事和 "tenbagger"
- Schloss：几乎不与管理层交谈，关注资产而非盈利

> Schloss 在被问及为何不像 Lynch 那样：**"He was killing himself. He was seeing 300 companies a year... We're not set up that way."**

### 5.4 Schloss vs. Charlie Munger

Munger 从未对 Schloss 公开发表评论。Munger 的"反捡烟蒂"立场（"The civilization is not going to reward people who spend their lives picking up used cigar butts"）与 Schloss 的方法截然相反。这种沉默有说服力——Munger 可能认为 Schloss 的方法过时，但尊重其记录。

## 6. 业绩记录分析

### 6.1 核心数字

| 时间段 | 指标 | Schloss (净) | S&P 500 | 倍数 |
|--------|------|-------------|---------|------|
| 1956-1984 (28.25年) | WJS 有限合伙人 | +16.1%/年 | +8.4%/年 | ~1.9x |
| 1956-1984 (28.25年) | WJS 合伙公司 | +21.3%/年 | +8.4%/年 | ~2.5x |
| 1955-2000 (45年) | 合伙公司 | +15.7%/年 | +11.2%/年 | ~1.4x |
| 1955-2002 (47年) | 毛回报（费用前） | +20.5%/年 | ~10%/年 | ~2x |
| 1955-2000 (45年) | 净回报（费用后） | ~15.3%/年 | ~10%/年 | ~1.5x |

**注意矛盾：** 不同来源对 Schloss 的回报率有差异，主要因为：
- 费用前 vs 费用后（Schloss 收 25% 利润分成，无管理费）
- 有限合伙人回报 vs 合伙公司整体回报（杠杆差异）
- 时间区间不一致（28年 vs 45年 vs 47年）

> **来源:** 巴菲特 1984 论文中的原始表格；Mr Deep Value 2025; West St Investing 2025; Fordham Gabelli Center 2021

### 6.2 风险指标

| 指标 | Schloss | S&P 500 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 下跌年份 (45年中) | 7 年 | 13 年 |
| 最大回撤 | -12.8% | 远大于此 |
| 破产个股占比 | 组合层面约 5%/年 | 市场约 2%/年 |

- **来源:** https://weststinvesting.substack.com/p/how-walter-schloss-beat-the-market

### 6.3 巴菲特证言：业绩不是由于少数大赢家

> "A few big winners did not account for his success." (2006 年致股东信)

这与许多人的直觉相反——Schloss 的收益来自"法律的大数定律"而非少数押注。

### 6.4 费用结构的影响

Schloss 不收管理费，只收 25% 的利润分成，且仅在盈利时收取。这意味着：
- 在下跌年份，投资者不支付费用
- 年化回报差距（费用前 20.5% vs 费用后 ~16%）巨大

对比：现代对冲基金收 2% 管理费 + 20% 业绩提成，无论盈亏。

## 7. 批评者的观点

### 7.1 Munger 的"捡烟蒂"批评

虽然 Munger 没有点名批评 Schloss，但他对捡烟蒂策略的批评广为人知：

> "The civilization is not going to reward people who spend their lives picking up used cigar butts."

> "It was a terrible mistake to buy Berkshire Hathaway when it was a cigar butt... Even if you buy it at a damn cheap price, you're going to have your capital tied up for a long time, and it hurts you a lot in terms of opportunity cost."

**隐含批评：** Schloss 的方法虽然有效，但机会成本高昂——本来可以买更好的企业获得更高回报。

### 7.2 有效市场理论的支持者

虽然巴菲特用 Schloss 的业绩反驳有效市场理论（EMT），但 EMT 的捍卫者可能会辩称：

1. **小市值偏差**：Schloss 主要投资于小盘股，而小盘股长期存在风险溢价
2. **流动性溢价**：Net-net 股票的流动性极差，高回报是对低流动性的补偿
3. **幸存者偏差**：可能有数百个尝试同样策略的人失败了，只有 Schloss 被记录
4. **运气因素**：虽然巴菲特说"不是运气"，但统计上总有人会长期获胜

### 7.3 策略的时代局限性

**批评一："捡烟蒂"依赖的套利空间在机构化市场中消失**

> "The locus of edge has shifted from information to time horizon." (Glen Bradford, 2026)

**批评二：市净率在无形资产时代的误导性**

> "Would be problematic to apply a simple price/book ratio in today's market, given the dominance of intangible assets." (Financial History, 2021)

**批评三：学术证据的可靠性受到质疑**

Alpha Architect 团队审查了 10 项 net-net 学术研究，结论：

> "Of the ten studies examined five suffered from inflated return bias (among other issues)... We therefore conclude that when viewed from the perspective of a practitioner, the empirical evidence analyzed is insufficient to support the view that investing in securities trading below their NCAV provides a reliable source of material outperformance."

> "the performance capability of such a strategy remains largely unknown from a practitioner's standpoint."

- **来源URL:** https://alphaarchitect.com/ben-grahams-net-current-asset-value-net-net-strategy/
- **可信度:** 高（Alpha Architect 以严谨方法论著称）

### 7.4 对"不与管理层交流"的批评

传统价值投资强调了解管理层的重要性。Schloss 不与管理层交流被有些人视为缺陷：

> "He was not a good judge of people" (Schloss 自己的话) —— 这意味着他放弃了投资分析中最重要的定性维度之一。

**隐含批评：** 若非自我设限，他的回报本可以更高。

### 7.5 巴菲特与 Schloss 的不同演化路径

虽然巴菲特从未批评 Schloss，但巴菲特自己的演化暗示了一种隐含批评：

巴菲特早期也做捡烟蒂（如巴菲特合伙公司时期），但随着规模增长和时间推移，他完全转向了"伟大企业合理价格"模式。他甚至在 1969 年关闭了合伙公司，部分原因是不再能找到捡烟蒂机会。如果连巴菲特都放弃了，这是否说明这个策略从根本上不可持续？

**矛盾记录：** Schloss 在巴菲特放弃后仍然用该策略获得了近 20 年的超额回报。这可能说明：
1. 巴菲特放弃是因为规模过大（当时已管理数亿美元）
2. 策略在特定规模下可行，但无法扩展
3. 20 世纪 80 年代后的市场仍然提供了捡烟蒂机会，只是巴菲特选择了更轻松的方式

## 8. 学术界关于 net-net 策略的研究

### 8.1 核心学术研究一览

| 研究 | 年份 | 样本期 | 核心发现 |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| **Oppenheimer, H.** "Ben Graham's Net Current Asset Values: A Performance Update" | 1986 | 1970-1983 | NCAV 组合年化 28.3% vs NYSE/AMEX 10.7% |
| **Greenblatt, et al.** | 1981 | 1930-1980 | NCAV 策略历史有效 |
| **Vu, J.** | 1988 | 延续 Oppenheimer | 策略持续有效 |
| **Lauterbach & Vu** | 1993 | 多市场 | NCAV 在国际市场中有效 |
| **Montier, J.** "Graham's Net-Nets: Outdated or Outstanding?" | 2009 | 1985-2007 | 全球 net-net 组合 35%/年 vs 等权市场 17%/年 |
| **Oxman, Mohanty, & Carlisle** "Deep Value Investing and Unexplained Returns" | 2011 | 1984-2008 | 月回报 2.55%，超额 1.66%/月；风险和因子无法解释全部 alpha |
| **Bildersee, et al.** | 1993 | 日本市场 | NCAV/MV 在日本市场有效 |
| **Xiao & Arnold** | 2008 | 英国市场 | NCAV 在英国有效 |
| **Alpha Architect (Merani)** 方法论批判 | 2019 | 多研究综述 | 多数研究中存在通胀回报偏差和不可投资偏差 |

### 8.2 Oppenheimer (1986) —— 开山之作

**方法：** 每年 12 月 31 日购买价格 <= 2/3 NCAV 的股票，持有 1 年，替换。

**结果：**
- NCAV 组合年增长率：**28.3%**
- NYSE/AMEX 基准：**10.7%**
- 小公司指数：**19.6%**
- 总计 645 个选股，最小年样本量 18 只

**缺陷：**
- 使用算术平均（非几何），可能夸大回报
- 小市值、低流动性股票
- 未完全考虑交易成本

- **来源:** https://alphaarchitect.com/an-analysis-of-benjamin-grahams-net-current-asset-values-a-performance-update/

### 8.3 Montier (2009) —— 全球验证

**方法：** 1985-2007 年，全球发达市场，买入 net-net

**结果：**
- Net-net 组合年化：**>35%**
- 等权市场组合：**~17%**
- 中位数组合含 65 只股票
- 只有 3 个亏损年份（市场有 6 个）
- 中位数市值仅 $2100 万

**发现的矛盾：**
- 个股层面：约 5% 的 net-net 股票年跌幅 >90%（市场仅 2%）
- 组合层面：表现优异
- 归因于行为偏差（窄框定、损失厌恶）

- **来源:** https://greenbackd.com/2010/01/21/montier-on-net-nets-a-simple-quantitative-value-strategy/

### 8.4 Oxman, Mohanty, & Carlisle (2011)

**关键发现：** 即使在控制了市场风险溢价、小公司溢价、价值溢价、动量、反转、流动性、一月效应之后，net-net 策略仍有无法解释的超额收益。

> "inclusion of these factors still does not explain the excess return available from the net nets strategy. Thus, we are left with a puzzle."

- **来源:** https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=1928694

### 8.5 Alpha Architect (2019) 方法论批判

审查了 10 项研究，只认可其中 1 项可靠。主要批判：

1. **通胀回报偏差**：使用算术平均而非几何平均
2. **不可投资偏差**：包括几乎无交易量的微型股
3. **幸存者偏差**：仅包括存续公司

结论：net-net 策略的有效性从实践者角度看"largely unknown"。

- **来源:** https://alphaarchitect.com/ben-grahams-net-current-asset-value-net-net-strategy/

### 8.6 最新实证：Benevolus Capital (2025)

2004-2024 年回测：
- 算术平均 +55%
- 几何/实际总回报 **-44%**
- 策略极度依赖市场时机（在 2008-2009 年大赚，其余大多数年份亏损）

- **来源:** https://benevoluscapital.com/2025/02/12/us-public-equity-research-backtesting-ben-grahams-net-net-investment-strategy-in-us-markets-from-2004-2024/

### 8.7 学术界的沉默——巴菲特的批评

巴菲特在 2006 年股东信中痛斥学术界无视 Schloss 的证据：

> "To my knowledge no business school teaching EMT made any attempt to study Walter's performance and what it meant for the school's cherished theory."

> "Instead, the faculties of the schools went merrily on their way presenting EMT as having the certainty of scripture."

## 9. 矛盾与张力记录

以下记录同一主题发现的不同甚至矛盾的陈述，**不进行调和**：

### 9.1 策略有效性

| 来源 | 立场 | 陈述 |
|------|------|------|
| Montier (2009) | 策略高度有效 | 全球 net-net 年化 35% |
| Alpha Architect (2019) | 证据不足 | 不可靠的研究方法论，从实践者角度策略有效性"largely unknown" |
| Benevolus Capital (2025) | 仅在危机时有效 | 2004-2024 年实际总回报 -44%，因为几何与算术的巨大差异 |
| Mr Deep Value (2025) | 仍然有效 | 适合小规模的个人投资者，行为偏差仍然存在 |

### 9.2 Schloss 是否改变了方法

| 来源 | 陈述 |
|------|------|
| Buffett (1984) | "And that's all he does" —— 暗示方法不变 |
| Schloss 本人 (1998) | "Yes, it's changed because the market's changed... I can't buy any working capital stocks anymore" |
| 追随者解读 | 核心原则不变（买低估），但具体指标从净运营资本转为市净率 |

### 9.3 与巴菲特的业绩对比

| 来源 | 说法 |
|------|------|
| Schloss 本人 | "we would have done better if we just had bought Berkshire Hathaway stock and held on" |
| Some analysts | 两人业绩相近，Schloss 的 15-16% vs 伯克希尔 22% |
| Investopedia | Schloss "20% per year returns for 50 years, standing shoulder to shoulder with Buffett" —— 这个说法可能混淆了费用前/后 |

### 9.4 是否需要与管理层交流

| 来源 | 立场 |
|------|------|
| Schloss | 不需要，因为不够擅长识人，且财务数据更客观 |
| Buffett | 至关重要——识人能力是他最大的优势之一 |
| Peter Lynch | 必须——每年拜访数百家公司 |
| Graham (理论) | 定量数据优先，但定性因素不能忽视 |

### 9.5 捡烟蒂策略的本质

| 来源 | 定义 |
|------|------|
| Buffett (比喻) | "cigar butt found on the street that has one puff left" —— 贬义 |
| Graham | "margin of safety" —— 褒义 |
| Munger | "a terrible mistake... opportunity cost" —— 强烈贬义 |
| Schloss | "We just try to buy cheap stocks" —— 中性/实用主义 |

### 9.6 回报数字的矛盾

不同来源对 Schloss 的回报记录不一致，原因包括：

| 来源 | 声称的回报 | 可能原因 |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Buffett (1984) | 合伙公司 21.3%/年 (1956-1984) | 费用前，含杠杆 |
| Mr Deep Value (2025) | ~15.3%/年净 (1955-2000) | 费用后，更长时间 |
| Fordham/MoAF (2021) | 20.5% 毛回报 (47年) | 费用前 |
| Investopedia | "20%/年 for 50 years" | 可能过高，混淆了不同口径 |
